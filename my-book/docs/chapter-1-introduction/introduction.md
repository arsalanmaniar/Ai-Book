# Introduction to Physical AI & Robotics
This module provides an overview of Physical AI and Humanoid Robotics, setting the foundation for advanced simulation and integration techniques.
## Learning Goals
After completing this module, students will be able to:
- Understand the fundamentals of Physical AI
- Explain the role of simulation in robotics development
- Identify key components of humanoid robotics systems
- Apply basic principles of robot simulation
